Special Treatments

Stone spa

Hot stone massage is a popular and therapeutic massage technique that involves the use of smooth, heated stones to enhance the traditional massage experience. 

Swedish

 Swedish massage is known for its ability to induce relaxation. The long, gliding strokes and gentle kneading help to calm the nervous system, reducing stress and promoting a sense of relaxation and well-being. The rhythmic strokes and kneading motions in Swedish massage can enhance blood circulation. This improved circulation can aid in the delivery of oxygen and nutrients to cells while also assisting in the removal of waste products from the body.

Deep tissue

Deep tissue massage is a therapeutic massage technique that focuses on reaching the deeper layers of muscles and connective tissues. It’s typically used to address specific musculoskeletal issues and chronic pain. The firm pressure applied during deep tissue massage helps release tight muscles and knots, promoting relaxation and increased flexibility.

Reflexology

 Reflexology is a holistic therapeutic practice that involves applying pressure to specific points on the feet, hands, or ears to promote relaxation, balance, and well-being throughout the body. Reflexology is believed to enhance blood flow and lymphatic circulation, which can help deliver oxygen and nutrients to cells while aiding in the removal of metabolic waste products from tissues.
